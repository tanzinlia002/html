VISION

Primeasia University is one of the leading private Universities in Bangladesh with a student-centered, community-engaged, comprehensive research wings distinguished by exceptional strength in science, technology and business.

MISSION

The mission of Primeasia University is to provide high quality, morally sound and socially relevant education, in the most cost-effective manner, to all its students, irrespective of gender, race, color, ethnicity or religion.
